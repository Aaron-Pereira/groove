▶ How this works

Adding music to your queue

  • Type Artist - Song Title for a single track, or Artist - Album Name for a full album.
  • Paste a YouTube URL directly to download from a specific video.
  • The worker downloads the audio, then beets matches it against MusicBrainz for proper tags and files it into your library.
  • High-priority requests jump ahead of bulk imports. Use Bulk Add for large batches.

Status meanings

  • pending – waiting in queue  |  searching – finding best match on YouTube
  • downloading – fetching audio  |  tagging – beets is matching + filing
  • done – in your library  |  failed – click Retry or supply a URL
