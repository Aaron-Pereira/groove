▶ How this works

Supported input formats (auto-detected)

  • Plain text – one entry per line: Artist - Album, Artist - Song, or a YouTube URL. Lines starting with # are ignored.
  • Spotify/Exportify CSV – export your liked songs at exportify.net, upload the .csv here. groove auto-detects the Spotify format.
  • Generic CSV – any CSV with artist/title/album columns (flexible header detection).
  • YouTube playlist URL – paste a playlist URL; groove expands it into individual tracks via yt-dlp.
  • Artist discography – type an artist name, pick albums from MusicBrainz, or use Queue all on the next page.

All bulk requests get priority: low so on-demand requests jump ahead. You'll see a dedup preview before anything is queued.
