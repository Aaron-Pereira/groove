Where discoveries come from

  • Daily: Billboard Hot 100, UK Official Top 40, Last.fm global chart, and your configured genre charts.
  • Weekly: New releases on MusicBrainz for artists in your Watchlist.

Auto-queue rules

Tracks that appear on {{ min_appearances }} or more charts this week are automatically queued at low priority. Adjust auto_queue.min_chart_appearances in groove.toml.

Actions

  • Add to queue – sends the track to the download queue at normal priority.
  • Dismiss – hides it from this view (archived nightly).
